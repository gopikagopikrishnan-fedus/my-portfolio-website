About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in Infocomm Technology (ICT) Cluster at Singapore Institute of Technolgoy (SIT), specializing in Distributed Lerning in Ultrasound Imaging, supported by the Ministry of Education Fellowship. I am affiliated with the SITxNVIDIA Centrre at SIT. Previously, I completed my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in 2024. I was previously working as Graduate Engineer Trainee at ALSTOM. I have been fortunate to participate in research internships at the Department of Electrical Communication Engineering, Indian Institute of Science (Summer 2022), at the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) and BPL Medical Technologies (Summer 2023), at the Multi-core ARchitecture and Systems (MARS) Lab, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati (Winter 2022).
